Common questions

What promoters and artists ask us most.

Ask us anything
Do I need a UAE work visa to book through ROSTR+? +

Visas remain the promoter's responsibility, same as any booking. ROSTR+ doesn't process visa paperwork, but the contract we generate captures who's responsible for which immigration step (entry permit, employment visa, exit). Most GCC promoters use a NOC + visit-visa pattern for single-event bookings; the contract template has fields for both, so the agreement is explicit before the artist boards a plane.

How do payouts work? Can artists receive AED directly? +

Yes. Promoters record payments on the platform (bank transfer reference, Stripe, or Tap). Artists confirm receipt with one tap. AED is the default; we also support USD and EUR for international artists. Payouts route directly between promoter and artist. ROSTR+ holds no funds. Every transaction has an invoice number, audit trail, and timestamp so VAT filings and dispute evidence are one click away.

What happens if an event gets cancelled? +

The contract you generate captures the cancellation policy at signing: typically 50% deposit non-refundable at >14 days, 100% inside 7 days. Both sides agree before signing. If a dispute arises, the full audit log (every message, status change, signature, payment record) is exportable as a single PDF for arbitration. We've kept the structure aligned with UAE Commercial Transactions Law so it holds up locally.

How does ROSTR+ verify artists? +

Three checks before a "Verified" badge appears: a working email or phone number, a real social presence (Instagram / SoundCloud / Spotify), and at least one cross-reference from a promoter or venue we already know. We don't verify performance quality (promoters decide that), but we do verify the human exists. Founding-50 artists go through the same checks.

Is ROSTR+ free? When does pricing change? +

Free during launch on both sides. No card required to start. When pricing turns on, we take 5–10% only from the promoter side, only after their third paid booking, and never from the artist. Founding-50 artists keep their badge and zero-commission rate forever. See the pricing section above for the structure.

What happens to my data? Is it private? +

Data lives on Supabase (EU-West-1 region) with row-level security on every table. Promoters see only their own bookings; artists see only theirs. Public profiles (name, bio, genre, rate band) are visible in the directory; private fields (email, phone) only surface to confirmed booking partners. Full details in our privacy policy, which is compliant with UAE PDPL.
